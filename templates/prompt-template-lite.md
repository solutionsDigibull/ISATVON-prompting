# ISATVON Lite Template (I + O + N)

For self-contained reasoning, coding, or one-shot questions with no constraints to enforce
and no external sources — the full 7 sections add cost without measurable quality gain
there. Copy everything below the line into any AI chat **as your message** and replace the
placeholders. If the task has constraints, sources, or a written deliverable, use
[prompt-template.md](prompt-template.md) instead.

---

## I — Instructions

You are [ROLE]. Your task: [ONE IMPERATIVE SENTENCE].
Rules: never invent figures or citations; if the task is impossible as stated, ask 1 clarifying question instead of guessing.

## O — Outcome

Structure your response as:

- **I** — the task as you understood it (1 sentence)
- **O** — the answer itself: [EXACT SHAPE — e.g. "the final number with 2–3 lines of working"]
- **N** — assumptions made, confidence (high/medium/low)

## N — Notification

In your N section, state every assumption you made and your confidence level.
